Rules

One player chooses an attack, other player defense

Keep on interchanging until a player has energy of 0

VS AI, player always attacks first


Attacks

Weak Attack: 0 to 5 damage

Strong Attack: 0 to 15 damage, attacker loses 5 energy

Fake Attack: counters Reflect, otherwise does 0 damage


Defenses

Reflect: reflects but loses 15 energy against Fake Attack

Risky Block: blocks -5 to 10 damage

Mistery Block: blocks 0 to 5 damage


Options

Text: enables/disables text about every move

Animations: enables/disables animations per round

Music: enables/disables music

Green is AI: allows you play versus AI
